Story of the game:

You are someone that suddenly woke up in the middle of nowhere whith a crashed space ship to your side, as you come to you realise what has happened but you don't quite know how you got on the spaceship and/or why you were on it in the first place.
You start learning how to survive by yourself, chopping down trees, mining rocks, finding other natural resources. You start to realise that you can repair the hyperdrive on your ship, and with that you set out on a journey to find all of the needed materials to repair it.
You will find many dangers and many other fun and or borin things, and then at the end you can finally repair your hyperdrive and return home... where ever that is.

Rules of the game:

  • there are no real "rules" to our game, it's go it's own boundries that are pretty self explanatory and some blocks that you can't break or walk through.
  • To walk around the world you just point somewhere and click, keep in mind that your character moves to the sides first then up and down.
  • You can select which item you have currently selected either with your mouse wheele or you can use the numbers on your keyboard 1 - 0.
  • To open the crafting menu you can press " i ".
  • Interacting with the world/items you can press the " e " key.
